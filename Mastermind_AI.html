Key features of our workshops

Expertise

Learn directly from AI industry experts who offer essential skills, latest trends, and valuable insights. Take advantage of their experiences and apply AI techniques to boost your organization or facilitate your career switch.

Hands-on Learning

Get your hands dirty with all the codings! Step-by-step guide for building, training, validating, and testing simple Machine Learning models or Neural Networks to solve real-world problems with real-world datasets.

Networking

Take advantage of our 30-min networking sessions and get to know others in the industry! Connect with like-minded people - be it someone in business or someone who is passionate about diving deep into Machine Learning algorithms. This is a great way to grow your network.
